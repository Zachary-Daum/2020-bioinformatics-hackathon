 In Switzerland, the situation is less well known. This article aims to
 present the evolution of the Swiss consumption and replace it in a European
 and international context using the International Narcotics Control Board
 database. Between 1985 and 2015, the Swiss opioid consumption raised from 18
 to 421 mg/person/year, making Switzerland the 7th largest opioid consumer per
 capita in the world. This increase could be due to more adequate pain
