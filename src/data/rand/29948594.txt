 cause extension of PR interval. Precautions are recommended when used in
 elderly and with other drugs extending PR interval. Cases of severe
 third-degree atrioventricular block have been reported only in post-marketing
 case reports when used at high-doses and remain rare. We report the case of
 an 88-year-old woman treated with bisoprolol, who experienced a complete
 atrioventricular block after initiation of lacosamide for epilepsy associated
