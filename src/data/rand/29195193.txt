 molecular rotors with 1,4-diethynylphenylene-d4 rotators are described. The
 ATR-FTIR and Raman spectra of acyclic rotor 3 and cyclic rotor 5E were
 measured and interpreted. A feature of ATR-FTIR spectrum of rotor 5E is a
 strong two-component band around 1730cm(-1) attributed to symmetric and
 asymmetric stretching vibration of the carbonyl group, while this is not
 observed in rotor 3. Raman investigation in the wide temperature range of 350
